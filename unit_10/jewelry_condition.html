About Our Jewelry

Posh Rx carries a collection of vintage fashion jewelry including such renowned names as Larry Vrba, Fendi, Valentino and Kenneth Jay Lane. Each piece arrives to us from auctions, antique flea markets, independent vendors and estate sales. All jewelry we carry is hand picked and carefully examined for defects before getting a stamp of approval for shipping to you. However it must be recognized that our beautiful items date back to 1950-1980's and, thus, a certain extent of natural wear is to be expected. Please do not hesitate to ask us questions via our contact form as we will gladly address any concerns pertaining to the condition, size and style of your desired item.
